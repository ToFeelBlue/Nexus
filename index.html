N E X U S !

Nexus is a highly organized and respected criminal group in CIT.
Founded on 21st January 2024 by JFX, with co-founders Walker and Ash, Nexus stands for power, discipline, and loyalty.
Nexus believes in building a tight-knit community where every member supports each other and contributes to our goals.
With strict rules and internal discipline, we ensure that all members remain focused, active, and respectful.
Our signature color is RGB(30, 240, 180) — symbolizing our energy, coordination, and impact.
Nexus has consistently maintained its position among the top reputation point holders in CIT.
Our unwavering dedication to teamwork, discipline, and strategic execution has earned
us the reputation of being one of the most respected and recognized criminal groups on the server — a true testament to our dominance in the criminal world of CIT.
If you're looking to be part of a skilled, respectful, and ambitious criminal family in CIT, Nexus is the place for you.

NEXUS: Group Rules & Guidelines

RULES

Nexus Rules

  • Observe all Nexus & CIT guidelines. Stay away from in-game punishments
  • Always show your group leaders respect and compliance. 20% caution
  • Never request raises, cash, or removal of a warning. 20% caution
  • Any rudeness shown against any member of the group could lead to an immediate Kick
  • Anyone who reaches the 100% warning level will be demoted immediately.
  • Don't be toxic to other people in team conversation; pay no attention to anything else. 30% level of caution
  • It is forbidden to provide any personal information about the group. 50% level of caution
  • In order to become inactive, you must first report your inactivity. 25% level of caution
  • Leaving the group without any notification will result in a Blacklist.
  • Getting a punishment as a trial will lead to you getting. Kick
  • Playing duels in Turf, will lead to get Kick

Entry Criteria

Join Conditions

  • A minimum of 300 hours ought to be attained.
  • You must be active In-Game.
  • A minimum of 5,000 kills are required.
  • You have to speak English rather well.
  • Don't apply if you belong to another group.
  • Should have a spotless punish-log, with no penalties issued in the previous 30 days.
  • Seven days after being rejected, you can reapply.
  • Shouldn't be known as a troublemaker or a toxic individual.
